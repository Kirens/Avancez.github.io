--- permalink: 1718/konstFuM/ layout: post title: "Konstituerande fullmäktigemöte 17/18!" date: 2017-05-24 23:00:00 +0100 categories: jekyll update --- _English below_ Ikväll hölls det konstituerande fullmäktigemötet, "FuM0", 18:00-23:30. Under detta möte valdes Talman, Vice Talman och 4 st personer till Valberedningen. Valet av övriga 4 st till Valberedningen hänskjöts till Kårstyrelsen. Om du är intresserad - hör då av dig till Valberedningen, Kårstyrelsen eller till oss i Avancez! --------------------- Tonight the Constituent Student Union Council meeting took place, "FuM0", 18:00-23:30. During this meeting the Speaker, the Deputy Speaker and 4 people were elected to the Nomination Committee. The election of the other 4 members to the Nomination Committee was referred to the Student Union Board. If you are interested, please contact the Nomination Committee, the Board or to us in Avancez!
